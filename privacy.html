Privacy Policy for Link Saver

Last updated: [Current Date]

Data Storage

Link Saver stores all data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking

Data Collection

The only data stored is what you explicitly save:

  • URLs of pages you choose to save
  • Page titles
  • Favicons
  • Tags you create
  • Favorite status of links

Data Access

All your data is stored locally in your browser and is only accessible by you. You can:

  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete any saved links
  • Clear all data by uninstalling the extension
